Passions - Creative & Emotional

What keeps catching your attention?

This can be something you want to help create, or an ongoing local gripe that still has not been properly solved.

For example: local arts, youth spaces, transport, digital help, gardens, care networks, events, clean-ups, signage, history, tourism, access, or small business support.

What keeps coming up in conversation, quietly annoying people, wasting time, or falling between the cracks?

This could be a proactive idea, a practical fix, a creative spark, something you already contribute to, or a problem you would join others to work on.

Ecosystem - Connection to the Whole

How do you link into island life?

Name the people, places, services, groups, tools, and informal networks that help you connect or contribute.

For example: ferries, buses, roads, halls, shops, schools, health services, beaches, tracks, phone reception, internet, waste, water, emergency services, or public spaces.

This might include family, neighbours, clubs, workplaces, cultural groups, volunteers, artists, carers, local businesses, online groups, or people you learn from. Only include what you want public.

Would you like to join a project, be contacted for certain kinds of help, share ideas, log public contributions, learn new tools, or simply stay in the loop?
